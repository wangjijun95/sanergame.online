About Saner Game

Saner Game is an immersive monster survival experience that challenges players to survive against waves of increasingly difficult creatures. Built with Unity, the game offers smooth gameplay and stunning visuals.

As a player, you'll need to strategize, upgrade your abilities, and master various weapons to survive the monster onslaught. Each wave brings new challenges and rewards, making every playthrough unique.

Controls

Progression

Earn experience points, unlock new abilities, and discover powerful weapons as you progress.
